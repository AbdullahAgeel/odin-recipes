Mbakbaka

Mbakbaka, a delicious libyan pasta dish you have probably never heard of

Mbakbaka is the dish I'm sure every libyan grew up eating (alot). It is an easy one pot pasta where all the ingredients cook together in one pot, and so the delicious flavours of the ingredients all fuse together to make this final masterpiece. Another amazing thing about mbakbaka is that it doesn't need many ingredients, just literally take whatever you have in your fridge and start cooking. If you have chicken then you can make chicken mbakbaka, if you have meat then you make meat mbakbaka and so on. So what are you waiting for, its delicious, quick, easy to cook and relatively unexpensive. Get cooking!

Ingredients:

  • 3 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 5 cups water
  • 2 tablespoons of salt
  • 1 teaspoon ground turmeric
  • 1/2 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 2 jalape√±os
  • The pasta of your choosing
  • 1 medium onion diced
  • 1 medium potato (cut into small cubes)
  • 2 minced garlic
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • chicken thighs/beef or lamb
  1. Add your olive oil into the pan
  2. Now once the olive oil starts to heat up add the diced onions and let them carmelize
  3. Once the onions have changed color and have begun to carmelize, add your choice of meat
  4. Lower the heat on the meat and place the lid on the pot and let it cook for 10 minutes
  5. Now add your tomato paste and mix the contents of the pan together
  6. Let the ingredients cook for 5 minutes and soak up the flavour from each other and then add your water to the mix
  7. Now add the tumeric, salt, paprika, chili powder, garlic, jalapenos and the potatoes to the mix
  8. Let them cook together for around 10 minutes
  9. Once the potatoes are cooked, add the pasta of your choosing
  10. Wait for the 8 minutes for the pasta to cook
  11. Now enjoy! Bonapetit!!
